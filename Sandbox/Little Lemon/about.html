About Us

Welcome to Little Lemon! We are a family-owned restaurant dedicated to bringing the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean to our community. Our menu features a delightful mix of Italian, Greek, and Turkish-inspired dishes, crafted from family recipes and fresh seasonal ingredients.

At Little Lemon, we believe in creating a warm and inviting atmosphere where you can enjoy a relaxed dining experience with friends and family. Whether you're in the mood for a light salad, a hearty pasta dish, or a refreshing lemonade, we've got something for everyone.

Our mission is to provide delicious food and exceptional service while fostering a sense of community. We look forward to welcoming you to Little Lemon and sharing our love for Mediterranean cuisine with you!


Our Story

Little Lemon began with two brothers, Mario and Adrian, who brought their passion for Mediterranean cuisine from Italy to Chicago. Inspired by family recipes, fresh ingredients, and the welcoming culture of Mediterranean dining, they created a restaurant that blends tradition with modern flavors.

From handcrafted pasta dishes to vibrant seasonal specials, Little Lemon was built to be a place where people can enjoy comforting meals in a warm and relaxed setting. Over time, the restaurant became known for its approachable atmosphere, rotating menu, and dedication to quality food made with care.


Meet the Team

The Little Lemon team is made up of chefs, servers, and creatives who share a love for Mediterranean food and hospitality. Every dish is prepared with attention to detail, using fresh ingredients and recipes inspired by Italian, Greek, and Turkish cuisine.

Mario leads the kitchen with his culinary experience and family-inspired cooking, while Adrian focuses on marketing, customer experience, and bringing new seasonal ideas to the restaurant. Together, they aim to create memorable dining experiences for every guest.


Community & Culture

At Little Lemon, food is more than just a meal — it is a way to bring people together. The restaurant regularly partners with local suppliers and celebrates seasonal ingredients inspired by Mediterranean traditions.

Whether guests are stopping by for lunch, dinner, or a casual gathering with friends, Little Lemon strives to create a welcoming space that feels connected to the community and the vibrant culture of Chicago.


Our Values

Little Lemon believes in fresh ingredients, honest cooking, and warm hospitality. Every menu item is thoughtfully prepared to balance traditional Mediterranean flavors with a modern dining experience.

The restaurant values quality, creativity, and consistency, while maintaining a relaxed atmosphere where guests can enjoy flavorful food at accessible prices. Sustainability, seasonal cooking, and genuine customer service remain at the heart of everything the team does.
